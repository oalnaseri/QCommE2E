# Channel Models

## Bosonic Gaussian Channel
Loss and additive noise modeled via beamsplitter transformation.

## Composite Channel
Cascades fiber impairments (PMD, chromatic dispersion) with quantum noise.

## FSO Turbulence
Malaga / Kolmogorov models for atmospheric fading.

## Depolarizing / Dephasing / Erasure
Standard qubit channels for discrete-variable benchmarking.
